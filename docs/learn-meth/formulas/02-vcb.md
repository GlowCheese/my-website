<div>
- $\sin u \sim u - \frac{u^3}6$
- $\sin^m u \sim u^m$
</div>

- $\tan u \sim u + \frac{u^3}3$
- $1 - \cos u \sim \frac{u^2}2$

<div>
- $ln(1+u) \sim u$
- $e^u - 1 \sim u$
</div>

- $\alpha^u - 1 \sim u \ln \alpha$
- $(1+u)^\alpha - 1 \sim \alpha u, \forall \alpha \ne 0$
- $\arcsin u \sim u$
- $\arctan u \sim u$
</div>
